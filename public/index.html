Watch the Yaw number. Turn your head left/right while sitting upright — if Yaw drifts or barely moves, that confirms the known blind spot: the setup turn can't be auto-detected and will need a manual "Ready" tap. Pitch and Roll are what drive the lying-down steps.

What each axis means for Epley

PitchReads lying back vs. sitting up — the biggest, most reliable signal in the maneuver.
RollReads turning the head while lying down and rolling onto your side. Also reliable.
YawCompass heading — jittery and drift-prone. We avoid depending on it.
